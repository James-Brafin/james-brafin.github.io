Lesser Beastshard, Hawk

Lesser Beastshard, Hawk is a form of Beastshard Spellglass which has the feathers of a Scraper Hawk inside it. This amber glass is cloudy, but retains some magical properties.

Effect

A wielder can expend a charge in this shard to gain a +2 circumstance bonus to their non-initiative Perception checks, as your eyes become sharper and more keen to pick out details. You can also see fine detail out to a distance of 300 feet, though you will lose close-up vision and periphery vision. This effect lasts for one minute.

When socketed in a weapon, you may expend a charge when you score a critical hit to deal an additional persistent 1d4 bleeding damage.

This shard has two charges, which recharge at dawn.
