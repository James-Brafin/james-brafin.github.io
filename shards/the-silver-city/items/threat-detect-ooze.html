Threat Detector Ooze

Threat Detector Ooze is a light violet ooze, which typically can be found in a small vial.

Effect

When introduced a fragment of a certain type of creature, the vial glows when any similar creatures (those that share a Trait) are within 60 feet, shedding dim light for 5 feet around the vial. This effect lasts for an hour, and then the vial is expended.

A vial can be spread across an item after being activated, generaly only taking a few moments. This imparts the same effects onto the item.
