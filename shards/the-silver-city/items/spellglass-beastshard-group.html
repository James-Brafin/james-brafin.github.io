Beastshard Spellglass

Table of Contents

  1. Introduction
  2. Properties
  3. Variants

Beastshard Spellglass is an amber-colored shard of Spellglass that has bits of an animal infused into it. Like most Spellglass, this shard can be used to cast spells, or to be socketed into weapons to provide effects to the blade.

Properties

Beastshard Spellglass, even in the weakest form, has access to a variety of spell-like effects depending on the creature embedded. While weaker fragments don't make for good sockets, stronger ones can provide weapon effects and can even be temporarily transformed into their chosen creature.
