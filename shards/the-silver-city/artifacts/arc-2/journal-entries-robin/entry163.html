Entry 163

With the sudden change in recent events, I've had to make do with staying at my lab for now. I'm not comfortable hanging around my apartment right now, and I can't stay on campus. I've set up a cot and brought some food over -- I know I shouldn't be using by burners for food prep, but I just wont tell Dr. Kromer. On the bright side, it's given me time to focus on a few experiments and work on some of my thesis.

While I'm here at my office hours, I'm leaving a spare key here in case I lose the one I've got for my lab. I know I shouldn't be copying it, but I don't want to get locked out if I lose my old one. At that point, I'd have to go back to the apartment.

I can't shake the feeling I'm being watched. I don't know why, but something is paying attention to me. Someone? I'm not sure. I'm mostly leaving the apartment for the weird dreams. I barely feel like I'm sleeping. All I see is pillars of stone and shadows, and I hear things all around me. I've never seen this place before. What is happening to me?
